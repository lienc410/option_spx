## Why was 2023-2026 down-tilted for BCD-eligible days?

Hypothesis: BCD-eligible days are LOW_VOL × BULL entry conditions. The 3y
period had several events where market entered LOW_VOL/BULL and then
quickly turned:

- 2023 Aug-Oct (~6% pullback after rally)
- 2024 Apr (Israel-Iran flare, -5% in days)
- 2024 Aug (carry trade unwind, -8.5% in 3 days)
- 2024 Dec (Fed surprise, -3% session)

Each of these had BCD entries shortly before the turn. The 26y baseline
includes longer stretches of sustained low-vol bull (2004-2006, 2013-2014,
2017) where BCD-eligible days were followed by sustained up windows.

Not all rolling periods will be this benign; 26y average includes
2007-2009 (gated out by matrix) and 2018, 2020, 2022 (also mostly
gated). The 26y forward profile we measure is **post-gate**, which is
the structurally correct sample for comparing future BCD performance.
